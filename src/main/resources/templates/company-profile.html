Introduce

Established in 2004, VNG has become the top Internet Company in Vietnam. Young, energetic and active, we are always ready for innovation with the “Embracing challenges” spirit to accomplish our ultimate mission of “Build Technologies and Grow People. For a better life”. VNG offers a wide range of products and services, categorized in 4 business groups: Online Games, Payment, Zalo, and Cloud, earning the trust of both customers and partners. We believe in the power of the Internet and see it as its mission to bring to Internet users meaningful experiences. Each day, millions of young Vietnamese entertain, connect, make friends, and engage in social activities through VNG products, which stands an ample testimony to how VNG creates value for the society. VNG is turning 15 yet we never cease “embracing challenges” in the next level. We would not take the old success as the goal for our new journey. Besides services and product development, growing people has become one of our primary missions, revolving around our 3 core values: embracing challenges, advancing partnership, and upholding integrity. We also brace ourselves across the Vietnamese border to take on the world stage.
